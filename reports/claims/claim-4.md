Do confirmed planets have different temperatures than the others ?
In this claim we will use koi_disposition for confirmation and koi_teq for temperatures .

We have two groups : 
Group A : Confirmed 
Group B : Not Confirmed (CANDIDATE OR FALSE POSITIVE)

As we know temperature can also influence how we determine if a stellar object is a planet or not.

My hypothesis : 

H0: There is no difference in temperature between the groups
H1: There is

For this claim i used a statistic test T.
The mean of temperature observed between the two groups is : -100.64 Kelvin.
The t-test statisitic is -5.86 leading to a p-value of 5.696928814488984e-09.

The p-value is less than 0.05, meaning there is a statistically significant difference in temperatures between confirmed and not confirmed planets. We can reject the null hypothesis that there is no difference in temperatures between the two groups.
