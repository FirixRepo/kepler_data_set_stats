But this won't answer my initial question ...

The goal here is to find if there is a relation between A planet's size and its orbital period.
After analyzing a scatter plot between the two variable, there seem to a relationship.
To make sure that it is the case i used correlation methods : 

Pearson's and Spearman correlation method.

After testing both of the methods, here are the results i got : 
Pearson correlation: 0.26489581637351656
Pearson p-value: 6.928204266459359e-58
Spearman correlation 0.4626995310149062
Spearman p-value 3.0213775929349617e-187


The result suggest that we have a positive medium correlation with the Spearman method, following the Spearman p-value that is close to 0 suggestion also that no correlation between size and orbital period would be rare under the null Hypothesis (H0: There is no correlation between a planet's size and its orbital distance).
